Icons Charlois Calendar

Calendar poster made for the Charlois Fashion Week. A community-driven fashion event in Rotterdam’s Charlois district organised by fashion designer Nada Van Dalen, that transforms local stories, people, and places into inclusive runway shows, workshops, and designs celebrating the neighborhood’s identity. The poster features 12 icons, Rotterdam-South locals who serve as inspirations and symbols for Nada’s collection. From the top, left to right: René, Gilbert, Angela, Dirk, Kadir, Van Es, Peter, The Hooks, Cihan, Willem. The calendar comes included with the Halte Charlois issue, featuring the inspiration material around the Charlois fashion week.

2D, 3D composition
59 × 42 cm
