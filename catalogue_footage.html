catalogue_footage

A catalogue featuring 20 books belonging to the best dutch books designs. I had access to the entire archive and decided to collect material in the most direct way I could think of to avoid being overwhelmed. I filmed the books as my friend Dragos flipped through them, unintentionally capturing the unique materiality and interactive properties of each work. Throughout the process, I experimented with a method of taking the least amount of choices possible, or at the very least, the dumbest ones. I refused to go through any more selection than I needed to, meaning I went ahead and used all 57 000 frames that resulted from the footage. I developed an indesign script to manage the placement of the frames and drilled through the stack to bind it.
