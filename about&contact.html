Franco-German and grew up many countries, which helped me develop a strong sense of cultural sensitivity and observation. I look for a story, an angle, an identity and express it in raw, sober outcomes, adapting to the respective context and needs.


Graphic design, image making, coding, photography, book design, print, blender, concept developement. Some of my work relates to fashion design, a connection that began with modelling on occasions to help out friends and colleagues.
