Waza's Bible Of No Manners

Naming and title sequence for a short documentary film my friend Kobus is working on. It follows Mich and his relationship with NEP (N-ethylpentylone), a designer drug that fuels his manic, fast-paced monologues and self-absorbed worldview. As Mich spirals deeper into his addiction, his confident, narcissistic energy gives way to moments of raw vulnerability.
