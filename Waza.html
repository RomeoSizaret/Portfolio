Waza's Bible Of No Manners

Thesis booklet I designed and produced for fashion designer Waza’s graduation project. The metal fastener idea, as raw as it may seem, came as he wanted 50 copies of this 90 page book, on a budget. The cover material is silver paper with a coat of laser printed red, which easily picks up scratches and wear, relating to Waza's use of experimental, rugged, distressed, and gritty materials.
