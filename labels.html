Jampot Labels

Jam pot labels for Aline. She noticed how her nephew had a label for his clementine farm, and she became a bit envious. She in turn wanted her own labels dedicated to her jams. I knew of her affinity for the countryside, birds, and plants. The background images were sourced from archival stock images under the themes of nature, rivers, mountains, countryside, Hawaii, Romania, and so on, to find a subtle nostalgic touch, maybe referring to a time were things seemed more simple. A few are of her garden, which she sent me. The ornaments come in as an interchangeable second layer, multiplying the possibilities of arrangements and allowing each label and its combination to be unique.
